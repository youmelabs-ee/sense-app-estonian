Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sense: Study Estonian collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address and login information
  • User ID used to identify your account
  • Learning progress, including learned and difficult words, statistics for correct/incorrect answers, and app preferences
  • Technical information such as device type, app version, and error logs
  • Any feedback submitted in app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your learning progress
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and maintain app security

3. Data Storage

Your data may be stored using secure third-party cloud services, including Supabase. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only when necessary to operate the app, such as authentication, database hosting, analytics, or crash reporting.

5. Account and Data Deletion

You may delete your account and associated data by clicking Delete Account button, in which case all your progress will be deleted without the possibilty to restore it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for general language learning use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law without parental consent.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
